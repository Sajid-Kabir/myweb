5. Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, SketchStore uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your device.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's device, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. SketchStore uses cookies to help SketchStore identify and track visitors, their usage of gd-sketchstore.web.app, and their website access preferences. SketchStore visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their device should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using SketchStore's websites, with the drawback that certain features of SketchStore's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to SketchStore's use of cookies.
